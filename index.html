Learn from the Greats

Get answers to your greatest questions from the greatest minds that have lived through history.

Richard Feynman

Richard Feynman is a highly regarded American theoretical physicist and a Noble Prize winner for developments in quantum electrodynamics.

Steve Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs was a American business magnate, pioneer of the personal computer revolution, and the chief executive officer of Apple Inc.

Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein is widely acknowledged to be one of the greatest physicists of all time. He is known for developing the theory of relativity and winning the Noble Prize for the law of the photoelectric effect.

Our unique process enables us to bring the greatest thinkers of history back to life. Here's how it works:

Gather Text

First, we obtain the question that the user wants to be answered.

Interpret Questions:

We then run the data through our proprietary machine learning model to interpret the question. This process invokes PyTorch's Natural Language Processing library to analyze the question for an answer.

Build Responses:

We handle the vast amounts of text written by the greats and collect it into a single corpus. This data comes in various formats, and we process it to be machine-readable. Our machine learning model then goes through a rigorous process of relating this data to recreate an answer to the question. The answer would then best approximate the actual response of the giant.

Enhance Learning:

Our stable server system would send the Machine Learning model's answer to the user.
